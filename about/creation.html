How To Create Your Own Page

Step 1: Head to github.com and create an account.

Step 2: Once you have created an account, on your homepage click on your user icon which should be in the upper right corner.
Click on the section labeled 'your repositories'.

Step 3: Find and click the icon that says 'New.'

Step 4: On the 'create new repository' page, label your new page <yourusername>.github.io

Step 5: Create your new repository!

Step 6: At this point, you can either create your own file or add/commit a file previously made.

Step 7: Once you have added your files you can now publish your page!
